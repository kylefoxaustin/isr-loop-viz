dual core

One fat core runs the inner 1 µs current loop and the outer RTOS loop today — and it works. Split them across two cores and the worry is: won't the outer loop stall the inner one? Watch the answer: on one core the outer load steals inner ticks; on two it only costs a little staleness — never a missed 1 µs deadline.
